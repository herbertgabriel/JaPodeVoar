Summary

The "Airlines Dataset to Predict a Delay" dataset provides information about flights, allowing for the analysis and prediction of delays. The dataset contains nine columns, including flight identifiers, airlines, origin and destination airports, day of the week, flight time, duration, and a delay indicator (0 for no delay and 1 for delay).
